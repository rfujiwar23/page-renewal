Speak It Live

Type: Web Development (Web App)

UI/UX, Coding, Front-End Development, Project Management

Link:
Official Link Currently Not Available
https://bit.ly/2F5CnBA (Figma)

Description:
This is a English-speaking web app created at LeWagon Coding Bootcamp in Tokyo, Japan. English teachers in universities carry multiple classes and also many students. It is very hard for them to keep track of individual students progress, especially when they have to remember their abilities on the spot.
This web app allows the student to record the conversation while in class, and the teacher grades the conversation as they speak (grammar, word choice, syntax, etc), and they are given homework based on their mistakes. The students are able to see their mistakes based on the time code, and fix their mistakes - and the teacher grades them through the app.
Please contact me to see the app in action.
